About WeedIN

WeedIN is a modern, responsible iOS app designed for cannabis enthusiasts who want to plan, learn, and connect while staying safe and informed. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive companion that promotes responsible cannabis use through education, community, and thoughtful design.

Whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or new to cannabis, WeedCircle helps you track your experiences, plan safe sessions, find legal dispensaries, and connect with a supportive community. We believe in the power of responsible cannabis use and are committed to providing tools that enhance your journey while prioritizing safety and privacy.
