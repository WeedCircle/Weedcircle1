Your Privacy Matters: WeedIN is designed with privacy-first principles. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Only: WeedIN operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Strain journal entries and ratings
  • Session plans and preferences
  • User profile information (nickname, location)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Community interactions (if used)

2. How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to or use any of your personal information. The app uses your local data to:

  • Display your strain journal and session history
  • Provide personalized dosage recommendations
  • Show relevant dispensary information
  • Customize your app experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in data protection mechanisms.

No Cloud Storage: We do not use cloud storage or external servers to store your information.

Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's security settings, including Face ID, Touch ID, and passcode protection if enabled.

4. Data Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.

No Analytics: We do not use analytics services or tracking tools that could identify you.

Optional Social Features: If you choose to use the community features, any information you share is done so voluntarily and is visible only to other app users you choose to connect with.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any information you've entered
  • Delete: You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Export: You can export your data through the app's export features

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete specific entries through the app
  • Uninstall the app from your device
  • Reset your device to factory settings

We do not have access to your data and therefore cannot delete it on your behalf.

7. Children's Privacy

WeedIN is not intended for use by individuals under 21 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 21. If you are under 21, please do not use this app.

8. Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with certain third-party services:

  • Maps Services: For dispensary location features (handled by Apple Maps)
  • App Store: For app updates and purchases (handled by Apple)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by posting the updated policy on our website. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@weedIN.app

Support: support@weedIN.app

11. Legal Basis for Processing

Since we do not collect or process any personal data on our servers, there is no legal basis for data processing to disclose. All data processing occurs locally on your device under your control.

12. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data remains within your device and jurisdiction.
