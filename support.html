Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new strain to my journal?

Tap the "+" button in the Strain Journal tab, then fill out the strain details including name, type, effects, flavors, and your personal rating.

Can I share my strain journal with friends?

Currently, your strain journal is private. You can share individual strains through the social features, but your complete journal remains private for your personal use.

How accurate is the dosage calculator?

Our dosage calculator provides general guidelines based on common factors. Always start with small amounts and consult with healthcare professionals for personalized advice.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, all your data is stored locally on your device and is private by default. We do not collect or share your personal information.

How do I find dispensaries in my area?

Use the Legal Map feature to find licensed dispensaries near you. The app will show ratings, contact information, and directions.

Can I use the app without creating an account?

Yes, WeedCircle works entirely offline and locally on your device. No account creation is required.

How do I plan a session with friends?

Go to the Session Planner tab, tap the "+" button, and fill in the session details including date, location, participants, and preferred strains.

What if I find incorrect information about a dispensary?

Please email us at support@weedcircle.app with the details, and we'll verify and update the information as soon as possible.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues with the app:

  1. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  2. Restart the app completely
  3. Check that your device has sufficient storage space
  4. Contact us with specific details about the issue
